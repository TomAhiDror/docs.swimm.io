# Swimm 0.9.0 Has Been Released!

We're pleased to announce the availability of **Swimm 0.9.0**<SwimmMoji text="release"/>

<SwimmReleaseBlogPost version="0.9.0" />

<SwimmReleaseVideo version="0.9.0" />

## New Features:
- Introducing our new Slack App to make it easier for you and your team to stay updated with Swimm! With the Swimm Slack app, once you connect your repo to a Slack channel, you will automatically be notified whenever a new doc is merged to your default branch. As an admin, just go to the Integrations page and click the Install button next to Slack.
- We now support documents and playlists list view. You can view more documents in the list view at once, with relevant information in each column. you can always switch back to the good old cards view.
- You can now control personalized Email, Slack and In-app notifications for when an important action affecting you occurred. The new Notifications area can be accessed from Profile > User Settings > Notifications.

## Improvements:
- App Facelift: We’ve made a few changes and improvements to Swimm's app. The Home page now shows workspace-related stats. All of your documents are still available under "Everything."
- Removed the Mark as Read button from docs (it is still available inside playlists).
- Improved Editor writing flow
  - Every line is draggable
  - Snippets, Images can now be inserted everywhere without limitations
- Improved Pasting of Markdown or HTML content
  - With new shortcuts (cmd+v | cmd+shift+v)
- Added a contextual menu to each line in the document
- New shortcuts moving content up or down (ctrl + shift + up | ctrl + shift + down)


For further information and any questions, please reach out to us on our <SwimmLink target="slack" />.
