# Swimm 0.8.4 Has Been Released!

We're pleased to announce the availability of **Swimm 0.8.4**<SwimmMoji text="release"/>

<SwimmReleaseBlogPost version="0.8.4" />

<SwimmReleaseVideo version="0.8.4" />


## Improvements:
 - Performance enhancements when connecting a repo to Swimm.
 - Auto-sync performance enhancements when viewing or editing a doc.
 - When viewing a doc, the last contributor is displayed in addition to the doc creator.
 - When viewing the Workspace Member's List and hovering over a user's name, the user's email is displayed.

## Bug Fixes:
 - Fixed missing repos issue.
 - Long branch names are correctly displayed via a tooltip.

For further information and any questions, please reach out to us on our <SwimmLink target="slack" />.
